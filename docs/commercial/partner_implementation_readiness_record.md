# Partner Implementation Readiness Record

**Package:** Commercial Scale Operating Model and Partner Implementation Readiness Pack  
**Evidence focus:** `SCALE-OPS-004`, `SCALE-OPS-007`, `SCALE-OPS-008`, `SCALE-OPS-010`

## 1. Partner Readiness Checklist

| Gate | Required evidence | Owner | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| Partner qualification | Partner capability, confidentiality, and access-boundary review | Partner Manager | Pending |
| Training readiness | AIM governance, evidence, AI staging, calculation, report, and work-order training record | Delivery Lead | Pending |
| Access scope | Least-privilege access request, approval, and revocation path | Security Owner | Pending |
| Customer data safety | Demo/sandbox/production-data boundary and redaction rules | Security Owner / Partner Manager | Pending |
| Evidence responsibility | Partner evidence capture and archive responsibility | Evidence Coordinator | Pending |
| Escalation path | Partner-to-AIM escalation route and SLA | Operations | Pending |
| Quality review | Human QA review before customer-facing closure | Lead Engineer / Product Owner | Pending |

## 2. Partner Authority Boundary

Partners may assist with implementation evidence collection, customer walkthroughs, configuration checklists, training, and support handoff only when explicitly authorized by named humans.

Partners cannot approve engineering data, AI promotion, calculation acceptance, report issue, work-order closure, customer acceptance, residual risk, security exceptions, production go-live, or scale operating-model closure.

## 3. AI/n8n/Service Actor Boundary

AI/n8n/service actors cannot approve partner implementation readiness.
AI/n8n/service actors cannot accept partner implementation evidence.
AI/n8n/service actors cannot approve partner access scope.
AI/n8n/service actors cannot accept scale operating risks.

n8n remains orchestration-only. AIM remains the system of record.

## 4. Evidence Safety Rule

Do not paste partner credentials, customer PII, real customer data, secrets, signed URLs, object-storage keys, contract redlines, partner contract terms, confidential sales pipeline data, invoice/payment details, tenant billing details, or payment processing data into this record.
