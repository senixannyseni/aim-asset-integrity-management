**Package:** Commercial Governance, Sales Enablement, and Scale Readiness Evidence Pack  
**Evidence focus:** `COMM-GOV-008`, `COMM-GOV-009`, `COMM-GOV-010`, `COMM-GOV-011`, `COMM-GOV-012`

## 1. Scale Readiness Checklist

| Scale area | Required evidence | Owner | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| Implementation playbook | Delivery steps, training model, support handoff, escalation path | Delivery Owner | Pending |
| Delivery capacity | Named delivery capacity, implementation limits, queue policy | Commercial Owner / Delivery Owner | Pending |
| Partner/channel policy | Partner access boundary, claims boundary, enablement material review | Commercial Owner / Security Owner | Pending |
| Support/SLA scalability | Support tier, escalation model, SLA/SLO assumptions, BAU owner | Operations / Customer Success Owner | Pending |
| Security/compliance scale | Access model, data handling, demo data safety, evidence retention | Security Owner / Compliance Owner | Pending |
| Continuous improvement link | Backlog link for scale gaps and enterprise readiness items | Product Owner | Pending |

## 2. Partner and Channel Boundary

Partner/channel readiness must be reviewed before any partner receives materials, demo access, customer evidence, implementation authority, or customer-facing commitments.

AI/n8n/service actors cannot approve partner/channel readiness.
AI/n8n/service actors cannot approve scale readiness.
AI/n8n/service actors cannot approve support handoff.
AI/n8n/service actors cannot accept commercial scale risks.
AI/n8n/service actors cannot sign commercial governance closure.

## 3. Support and SLA Scale Boundary

Support/SLA scale readiness must be based on named human capacity, approved escalation rules, and realistic operational evidence. AI/n8n/service actors cannot approve SLA exceptions.

## 4. Commercial Scale Decision

| Decision option | Meaning | Required owner |
|---|---|---|
| Approve controlled scale | Evidence is complete and risks are accepted by named humans | Product Owner / Commercial Owner |
| Approve limited scale | Scale allowed only for defined customer/segment/partner constraints | Product Owner / Commercial Owner |
| Defer scale | Evidence or capacity gaps remain | Product Owner / Commercial Owner |
| Block scale | No-go condition exists | Product Owner / Commercial Owner |

Do not paste secrets, customer PII, real customer data, partner contract terms, pricing exceptions, customer commercial terms, invoice/payment details, tenant billing details, payment processing data, or confidential pipeline data into this record.
