## 4. Rollback and Offboarding

Rollback/offboarding must include customer communication owner, data export/deletion decision, access disablement, evidence archive owner, and support handoff.

AI/n8n/service actors cannot authorize customer rollback or offboarding.

Do not paste secrets, JWTs, passwords, object-storage keys, signed URLs, production credentials, webhook secrets, CMMS credentials, database connection strings with passwords, private keys, confidential client evidence, real customer data, customer PII, customer commercial terms, contract redlines, invoice/payment details, or legal opinions into this record.

n8n remains orchestration-only. AIM remains the system of record.
