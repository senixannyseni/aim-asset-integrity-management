| Baseline area | Required evidence | Status | Owner |

## 2. Closure Decision Options

- Close commercial evidence-control baseline and move to governed enterprise-scale roadmap;
- close with carryover items in the enterprise-scale backlog;
- extend commercial stabilization with named owners and target dates;
- record no-go if baseline evidence is incomplete or unsafe.

## 3. Human-Only Closure Authority

Final commercial closure requires named human approval from Product Owner, Operations, Commercial/Sales Owner, Customer Success Owner, Security Owner, and leadership/business sponsor when enterprise expansion is planned.

AI/n8n/service actors cannot accept commercial final closure evidence.
AI/n8n/service actors cannot sign commercial final closure.
AI/n8n/service actors cannot waive missing commercial final evidence.
AI/n8n/service actors cannot close commercial final closure gaps.

n8n remains orchestration-only. AIM remains the system of record.
