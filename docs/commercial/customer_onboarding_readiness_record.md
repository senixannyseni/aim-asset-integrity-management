# Customer Onboarding Readiness Record

**Package:** Commercial MVP Launch Control and Customer Onboarding Evidence Pack  
**Evidence focus:** `COMM-LAUNCH-003`, `COMM-LAUNCH-004`, `COMM-LAUNCH-005`, `COMM-LAUNCH-006`, `COMM-LAUNCH-008`, `COMM-LAUNCH-009`

## 1. Customer Fit and Qualification

| Check | Required evidence | Status | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Target customer profile | Industry/use case fit, buyer, user roles, pilot scope | Pending | Commercial Owner |
| Technical prerequisites | Deployment model, browser/network needs, data/evidence flow | Pending | Lead Engineer |
| Governance fit | Human review, evidence linkage, calculation/report/work-order gates accepted by customer | Pending | Product Owner |
| Exclusions acknowledged | Out-of-scope APIs/features/formulas/integrations documented | Pending | Product Owner |

## 2. Onboarding Plan

| Step | Evidence | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Kickoff | Customer stakeholder list, success criteria, and onboarding calendar | Customer Success |
| Access setup | Named user roles, least-privilege access, and admin ownership | Security Owner |
| Training | Training agenda, attendance, and role-specific workflow walkthrough | Product Owner |
| UAT/customer validation | Acceptance criteria, scenarios, defect path, and signoff | Customer Success |
| Support activation | Helpdesk/contact path, SLA, escalation, and incident categories | Operations |

## 3. Demo/Sandbox and Data Safety

Do not paste secrets, JWTs, passwords, object-storage keys, signed URLs, production credentials, customer commercial terms, legal opinions, raw vulnerability exploit details, real customer data, contract redlines, invoice/payment details, or customer PII into this record.

Demo/sandbox onboarding must use synthetic data or approved redacted evidence only. Real customer evidence must be stored only in approved secure evidence storage.

## 4. Human Authority Boundary

AI/n8n/service actors cannot approve customer onboarding.  
AI/n8n/service actors cannot approve customer acceptance.  
AI/n8n/service actors cannot waive customer onboarding evidence.  
AI/n8n/service actors cannot accept customer onboarding risks.

n8n remains orchestration-only. AIM remains the system of record.
