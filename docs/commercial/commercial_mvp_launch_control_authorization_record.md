# Commercial MVP Launch Control Authorization Record

**Package:** Commercial MVP Launch Control and Customer Onboarding Evidence Pack  
**Evidence focus:** `COMM-LAUNCH-001`, `COMM-LAUNCH-002`, `COMM-LAUNCH-010`, `COMM-LAUNCH-012`

## 1. Launch Baseline

| Baseline item | Required evidence | Status | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Productization roadmap tag | Tag, commit SHA, PR, and evidence archive reference | Pending | Product Owner |
| Commercial launch scope | Included/excluded modules and buyer/user profile | Pending | Commercial Owner |
| Runtime exclusions | No unapproved runtime/API/DB/formula/AI/n8n/object-storage changes | Pending | Lead Engineer |
| Evidence archive | Link to approved evidence storage | Pending | Evidence Coordinator |

## 2. Launch Authority

| Role | Authority | Required signoff |
|---|---|---|
| Product Owner | Product scope and launch/no-go decision | Required |
| Commercial Owner | Commercial launch readiness and customer-facing commitments | Required |
| Lead Engineer | Technical baseline and release/change-control boundary | Required |
| Security Owner | Security/legal/compliance readiness or no-go | Required when security/legal/compliance items are open |
| Operations / Customer Success | Support, onboarding, incident, and communication readiness | Required |

## 3. Decision Options

- Approve controlled commercial MVP launch;
- Approve conditional launch with named owners and dates;
- Defer launch pending customer, legal, security, support, or technical evidence;
- Block launch and return to productization backlog/change control.

## 4. Human-Only Launch Authorization

AI/n8n/service actors cannot approve commercial launch.  
AI/n8n/service actors cannot accept commercial launch evidence.  
AI/n8n/service actors cannot accept commercial launch risks.  
AI/n8n/service actors cannot sign commercial launch authorization.

n8n remains orchestration-only. AIM remains the system of record.
