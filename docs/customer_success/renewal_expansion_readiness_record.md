# Renewal and Expansion Readiness Record

**Package:** Customer Success, Commercial Operations, and Renewal Readiness Evidence Pack  
**Evidence focus:** `CS-OPS-008`, `CS-OPS-009`, `CS-OPS-010`, `CS-OPS-012`

## 1. Renewal Readiness Checklist

| Gate | Required evidence | Owner | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| Customer health reviewed | Green/yellow/red score and rationale | Customer Success Owner | Pending |
| Adoption/value evidence | Workflow usage, value realization, success metrics | Customer Success Owner / Product Owner | Pending |
| Support/SLA status | Open support items, exceptions, and customer impact | Support Owner | Pending |
| Security/compliance items | Access, confidentiality, evidence safety, legal/compliance carryover | Security Owner / Commercial Owner | Pending |
| Residual risks | Customer lifecycle risk register with owner and target date | Product Owner | Pending |
| Renewal decision | Continue, renew, expand, stabilize, pause, or stop | Commercial Owner / Product Owner | Pending |

## 2. Expansion Readiness Checklist

Expansion readiness is evidence-control only. Expansion does not authorize unapproved runtime features, tenant billing, payment processing, full API 579, full API 581, 3D processing, copied API/API-ASME formulas, or external CMMS implementation.

| Expansion area | Required evidence | Owner | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| Expansion signal | Customer need, use case, and value hypothesis | Commercial Owner | Pending |
| Scope boundary | Included/excluded modules and governance gates | Product Owner | Pending |
| Technical dependency | Runtime, security, integration, data, or support dependency | Lead Engineer | Pending |
| Commercial dependency | Pricing/licensing/legal assumptions | Commercial Owner | Pending |
| Human decision | Approved, deferred, rejected, or moved to backlog | Product Owner / Commercial Owner | Pending |

## 4. Human Authority Boundary

AI/n8n/service actors cannot approve renewal readiness.  
AI/n8n/service actors cannot approve expansion readiness.  
AI/n8n/service actors cannot accept customer lifecycle risks.  
AI/n8n/service actors cannot approve renewal decisions.  
AI/n8n/service actors cannot approve expansion scope.  
AI/n8n/service actors cannot sign customer lifecycle closure.

AIM remains the system of record. n8n remains orchestration-only.
